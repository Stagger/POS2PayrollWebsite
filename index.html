No More Manual Payroll Data Entry

POS2Payroll collects the payroll data from your POS system and prepares it for import to your payroll provider without the need for manual data entry.

Cloud Based Technology

Our product pushes the necessary information from your POS into the cloud. When it's time to run payroll, use any computer to login and pull your payroll data from the cloud.

Avoid Payroll Errors

By automating the payroll process you can avoid errors related to manual entry of payroll data.

Complete Searchable Records

POS2Payroll also offers archiving services so your accountants and payroll providers can access essential data quickly and easily.
